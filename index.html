2. What is a Cricket ID?

A Cricket ID is a unique identification number assigned to players, coaches, and officials involved in the sport. It serves multiple purposes, including:

  • Player Identification: Ensures that each player is easily recognizable and verifiable.
  • Match Registration: Facilitates the registration of players for different leagues, tournaments, and matches.
  • Data Management: Helps in maintaining accurate records of player statistics, achievements, and performance history.
  • Compliance and Regulation: Assists in adhering to regulatory standards and norms set by cricketing authorities.
